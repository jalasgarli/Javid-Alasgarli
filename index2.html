Hour of Code Project

Team 404! has successfully conducted the "Hour of Code" project at Sumqayit Secondary School No.1. Our team wanted to show that anyone can learn the basics of coding. Together with 30 students of 7th grade, we completed 20 exercises that helped them to nurture their problem-solving skills, logic, and creativity.

We set certain goals we aimed to meet by the end of the project and discussed several topics related to the technology world. Also, our members gave students introductory information about the basics of coding. After that, they started to solve the puzzle problems presented in a game form that contained various blocks of codes. Students not only had fun solving these problems but also learned new concepts. Together, we solved all the problems of varying complexity.
